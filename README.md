Chaperon

Chaperon is a smart travel companion that helps travelers quickly understand and explore any city in the world.
Instead of searching through multiple websites, Chaperon provides a curated overview of a city’s history, culture, attractions, and practical travel information—all in one place.

The goal of Chaperon is to make travel simpler, smarter, and more immersive.

✨ Features
🏙 City Overview

City Bio – basic facts such as population, language, currency, and time zone

Brief History – a concise historical overview of the city

City Vibe – a snapshot of the atmosphere, lifestyle, and culture

🌍 Discovery

Top Attractions – must-see landmarks and popular destinations

Hidden Gems – secret spots and lesser-known locations

Interesting Facts – unique and surprising details about the city

Nearby Destinations – interesting places within ~2 hours of the city

🍴 Culture & Lifestyle

Local Cuisine – traditional dishes and recommended food experiences

Events & Festivals – current or upcoming local events

Local Tips & Etiquette – cultural norms and advice

Do’s and Don’ts – helpful guidance for respectful travel

🗣 Language

Useful Phrases – essential phrases in the local language to help travelers communicate

🧭 Navigation

Integrated Map – explore attractions and points of interest visually

Transportation Information

Public transport schedules

Airport information

Taxi services

🧳 Travel Utilities

Weather Reports – current conditions and forecasts

Currency Converter – live exchange rates

Emergency Numbers – important local contacts for safety

🚀 Vision

Chaperon aims to become an all-in-one travel intelligence platform that combines:

city knowledge

travel planning tools

local insights

real-time utilities

Future versions may include:

AI-powered travel recommendations

personalized itineraries

offline city guides

traveler community features

AR exploration tools

📦 Installation

Clone the repository:

git clone https://github.com/yourusername/chaperon.git
cd chaperon

Install dependencies:

npm install

Run the development server:

npm run dev
